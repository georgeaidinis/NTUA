SOPHIA Tool Disclaimer
Welcome to SOPHIA Tool!

SOPHIA Tool is a company-wide skills management system for internal use, enabling registration of own public skills and visibility over the public skills of the employees that allow this action, via a well-defined permission system.

The use of the SOPHIA Tool is voluntary for employees and refraining from it will cause no detrimental or disciplinary consequences.

Personal information, and notably skills are controlled by the employee himself/herself. The employee can register ONLY the public skills he/she wants so. Employees can choose anytime to remove their skills’ information from the Tool.

No secret or confidential content are stored in the SOPHIA Tool in any format (documents, discussions, private messages, etc.).

Users must adhere to Nokia policies, including Nokia’s Information Security Policy, National Security Agreement Policies, and the Nokia Code of Conduct in their use of the SOPHIA Tool. Violation of Nokia Policies or the Nokia Code of Conduct or associated procedures may be regarded as a reason for disciplinary actions up to termination of employment.

Permissions :
  • The solid line manager can see the public skills of own employees.
  • Each employee can see the statistics of own-location employees down to team level, without names.
  • Authorized Tool administrators have access to the public skills of all employees.
